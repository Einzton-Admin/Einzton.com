About Us

Einzton Technologies is an “Offshore Software Development Company”, headquartered in Chennai, India. We possess the proven experience of deploying and running secure dedicated software development center, along with delivering large and mission-critical projects. Einzton Technologies follows proven industry standard process, reusable frameworks, professional wisdom and ready infrastructure.

At Einzton Technologies, our focus is on quality offshore software development, and in this process we continually educate and keep ourselves acquainted with the latest concepts, tools and technologies. In short, it’s our constant endeavor to uphold the academic talent required to meet the business objectives.

We have state-of-the-art offshore development center where we maintain an array of multidisciplinary team with core capabilities. We offer our clients the benefits of quality; yet cost effective software solutions delivered by our team of experienced professionals. In short, our offshore software development center acts as an extension to our global clientele where we aim at creating associations with our clients who seek long-standing services.
